Chandragupta Maurya

ಚಂದ್ರಗುಪ್ತ ಮೌರ್ಯ: ಭಾರತದ ಪ್ರಥಮ ಸಾಮ್ರಾಟ

Founder of the Mauryan Empire

(322–298 BCE)

Scroll Sideways →

I. Origins & Mentor

ಉಗಮ ಮತ್ತು ಗುರು
  • Overthrew Dhana Nanda (Last Nanda King) to establish the Dynasty.
  • Guided by Chanakya (Kautilya/Vishnugupta), the master strategist.
  • Known as Sandrocottus in Greek records.
  • Capital: Pataliputra (modern Patna).
✦ Note: The Sanskrit play Mudrarakshasa (by Vishakhadatta) details his rise to power.

II. Conquests & Rule

ದಿಗ್ವಿಜಯಗಳು ಮತ್ತು ಆಡಳಿತ
  • Defeated the Greek General Seleucus Nicator (305 BCE).
  • Acquired territories: Kabul, Kandahar, Herat, and Baluchistan.
  • First ruler to unite almost the entire Indian Subcontinent.

III. Faith & End

ಧರ್ಮ ಮತ್ತು ಅಂತಿಮ ದಿನಗಳು
  • Embraced Jainism in his later years.
  • Followed the Jain monk Bhadrabahu to South India.
  • Abdicated the throne for his son Bindusara.
  • Performed Sallekhana (fasting to death) in Karnataka.
✦ High Alert: The place of his death is Shravanabelagola (Hassan Dist, Karnataka). The hill is named Chandragiri after him.
